HRS HLX-090 Helix isolators are fully compatible with CH Precision L10, P10, D1.5, C1.2, and T1 units when placed on HRS SXR racks and M3X bases, provided they are positioned at the four chassis corners where the original feet reside. The casework is engineered to bear weight only at these corners, and replacing factory spikes with Helix pucks is recommended—especially on granite-topped M3X bases—due to superior vibration isolation and energy transfer on reflective surfaces. Factory feet on CH Precision units are uniquely placed for structural and vibration reasons, so Helix pucks must mimic the same layout; never run both spike and Helix drain paths simultaneously. Employ 4 Helix pucks per main unit (3 for T1), keeping spikes retracted or removed as needed for optimal performance.

Key points:
- Helix pucks replace CH spikes at corners, never both in contact
- Always position Helix pucks where factory feet were installed
- Designed for use atop HRS M3X bases with granite surfaces
- Corner-only support matches CH Precision's load and energy drain path design
